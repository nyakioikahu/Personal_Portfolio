My Personal Story:

Growing up, I was always drawn to the thrill of solving puzzles and unraveling mysteries. One of the most memorable experiences was a large-scale treasure hunt organized by Parklands Sports Club. With competitors of all ages, it was an exhilarating race that required sharp thinking and quick decision-making. During the event, each clue presented a puzzle that demanded a combination of lateral thinking and logical deduction. I remember one challenge where I solved a coded message by spotting common patterns and linking them to the event's theme, which led me to the next location. My efforts paid off, and I emerged as the winner of the treasure hunt, earning an unforgettable trip to Mombasa. This experience solidified my belief in the power of combining logic and creativity to overcome challenges.
